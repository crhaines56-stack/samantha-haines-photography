# Introducing Vue Studio: Austin's Newest Portrait Photography Studio

Something we've been quietly building for a long time is finally ready to share.

Vue Studio — Samantha Haines Photography's dedicated portrait studio — is now open at **2302 Jacks Pass, Austin TX**. This is the space we designed to give every client the kind of session that wasn't possible before: fully controlled, beautifully set, and endlessly flexible.

Here's what you need to know.

## What Is Vue Studio?

Vue Studio is a 2,500 square foot, purpose-built portrait photography studio in Austin, TX. It was designed from the ground up with one goal: to give every client — regardless of what kind of session they're booking — a premium, professional environment that removes every limitation outdoor shooting brings.

No weather cancellations. No changing light. No crowds. No compromises.

Whether you're booking a [boudoir session](/boudoir-photographer-austin), [senior portraits](/senior-portrait-photographer-austin), [family photos](/family-photographer-austin), [newborn photography](/newborn-photographer-austin), [maternity portraits](/maternity-photographer-austin), or [headshots and branding](/headshots-branding-photographer-austin) — Vue Studio has a set built for you.

## The Sets

Vue Studio houses **8 fully furnished sets**, each with its own distinct look, feel, and lighting environment. From clean and minimal to warm and editorial, each set is designed to give clients maximum visual variety within a single session.

Sets include dedicated areas for:

- **Boudoir and intimate portraiture** — rich textures, layered lighting, privacy-first design
- **Newborn and maternity** — safe, temperature-controlled, calming environments
- **Senior and lifestyle** — versatile backdrops from editorial clean to lifestyle-warm
- **Business and branding** — professional environments appropriate for headshots, team photos, and content creation

The studio also features **motorized backdrop systems** — meaning we can swap looks in seconds, not minutes. More looks, more options, more value from every session.

## Hair & Makeup

Vue Studio has a dedicated **hair and makeup suite** on-site, with professional styling stations and salon-quality lighting. Our in-house hair and makeup artists are available for booking with any session — boudoir, senior, maternity, headshots, or otherwise.

Getting ready on-site means you arrive camera-ready without the stress of coordinating schedules or worrying about whether your makeup will hold through the drive. You step out of the chair and directly into the studio. It's seamless.
