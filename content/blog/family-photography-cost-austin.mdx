## The Austin Family Photography Price Range

At the budget end—$150 to $400—you're typically getting a solo photographer, maybe a 30-minute session at a park, and a gallery of digital files. There's nothing wrong with that if a casual snapshot is what you're after. The images might be perfectly pleasant. They just probably won't be framed on your wall five years from now.

Mid-range photographers in Austin—$500 to $1,500—bring more experience, better equipment, and a more polished process. You'll see more consistency in the editing, better posing guidance, maybe a wider variety of looks within the session. The gap between this tier and the budget tier is real and noticeable.

Then there's the premium tier—$2,000 and up. This is where the entire *experience* changes. It's not just a photographer. It's a production. It's an investment in artwork, not snapshots.
