Most photographers don't offer a guarantee.

There are a lot of ways to explain that. Photography is subjective. Art is hard to guarantee. Every client is different.

Those things are true. They're also convenient.

Here's why we offer one.

## What the Guarantee Says

It's simple:

> *If we come to the end of the experience and you've reviewed your final images and it's not love — we'll re-edit, re-shoot, or refund.*
>
> No questions asked. No fine print.

That's it. No asterisks. No "satisfaction guaranteed subject to the following conditions." No paragraph of exclusions that renders the guarantee meaningless.

If the images aren't love, we make it right.

## Why It Exists

The Happiness Guarantee exists because of what it costs to book a session.

Not just financially — though portrait photography is an investment. It also costs something emotionally. Especially for boudoir clients, who are putting themselves in front of a camera in a way they've never done before. It costs something in vulnerability. In trust.

When someone books a session, they're not just purchasing photographs. They're deciding to trust someone with something that matters to them. Their body. Their family. Their milestone. Their story.

A guarantee says: *I take that seriously. Your trust is not something I'm willing to squander.*

It also creates alignment. When a photographer knows they're responsible for the outcome — not just the process — they approach every session differently. Every consultation becomes more thorough. Every posing decision becomes more intentional. Every editing choice matters more. You're not just clicking a shutter and delivering files. You're delivering on a promise.

## What It Covers

If you review your final images and something isn't right, here's how the guarantee works:

**Re-edit.** If the editing isn't what you envisioned — the tones, the retouching, the overall look and feel — Samantha will re-edit. You'll describe what you wanted, and she'll get there.

**Re-shoot.** If the images themselves don't capture what you hoped for — if the session didn't go the way either of you planned — Samantha will schedule a re-shoot. A new session, at no additional charge.

**Refund.** In the rare case where neither of the above resolves things, you'll receive a refund.

Which path makes sense depends on what happened. Samantha handles that conversation personally, not through an assistant or a form.

## Why It Rarely Gets Used

The guarantee exists as a safety net. But it's rarely needed — not because it's buried somewhere in the contract, but because of how the experience is built.

Every session begins with a consultation. This isn't a 10-minute intake call. It's a real conversation about what you want, what you're nervous about, what your vision is, and how to get there. By the time a client arrives for their session, Samantha knows them well enough to direct them with confidence.

Hair and makeup are included in every session. Clients don't show up having stressed about styling for an hour — they arrive relaxed and ready, handled by a professional artist who knows how to prepare someone to be photographed.

The sessions themselves are unhurried. There's no rushing through looks because the clock is running out. Time is built in for settling, for connection, for finding the moments that actually feel real rather than just posed.

All of this means the gap between "what you hoped for" and "what you received" is very small. The guarantee is there for the rare case where something doesn't land despite all of that. It's the final layer of a process designed from the start to make it unnecessary.

## What It Says About How We Work

There are two ways to run a photography business.

One is to create distance between expectation and responsibility. Keep the experience opaque. Limit revisions. Deliver files and move on. Protect the business from the uncertainty of human taste.

The other is to take full ownership of the result. To stand behind the work completely. To treat every client's satisfaction not as a variable you can't control, but as an outcome you're accountable for.

The Happiness Guarantee is how we tell clients which kind of business this is — before they've even booked.
